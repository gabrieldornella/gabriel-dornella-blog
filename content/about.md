I am a technology executive based in São Paulo, Brazil, with more than 20 years of experience in enterprise software, solution engineering, technical architecture, data, CRM, and AI across Latin America.

My career has been built at the intersection of technology, business value, and customer transformation. I have worked at companies such as monday.com, Salesforce, SAP, Oracle, and Hyperion, moving through roles in technical consulting, pre-sales, account management, and regional leadership.

Today, I lead Solution Engineering for Latin America at [monday.com](https://monday.com/), where I manage the regional team of Solution Engineers. Our mission is to stay close to customers, understand their strategic priorities, and help them turn business objectives into practical workflows, scalable solutions, and measurable outcomes.

I see monday.com as one of the most compelling AI platforms for work because it connects the way teams plan, collaborate, automate, and execute. My role is to help customers understand that potential in practical terms, while also supporting the growth of the business across Latin America.

Before monday.com, I spent several years at Salesforce, where I held leadership roles including Senior Director of Architecture, Data & AI for Latin America and Senior Director of Solution Engineering in Brazil. I led teams of solution engineers, technical architects, and enterprise architects supporting large companies across the region.

A significant part of my work has been helping organizations translate strategic priorities into technology decisions. That includes executive conversations, current-state assessments, future-state architecture, transformation roadmaps, business cases, and adoption strategies around data, CRM, AI, and digital transformation.

Earlier in my career, I held sales roles at Salesforce and sales/pre-sales roles at SAP, Oracle, and Hyperion, working with enterprise customers across industries such as financial services, manufacturing, communications, media, higher education, professional services, real estate, engineering, and construction.

Those experiences shaped how I think about enterprise technology. The best solutions are not only technically strong. They are understandable, adopted by people, connected to business priorities, and capable of creating real impact.
